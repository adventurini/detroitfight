Privacy Policy

Detroit Fight Company · 1425 Tuskawilla Rd #209, Winter Springs, FL 32708

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Detroit Fight LLC, doing business as Detroit Fight Company (“we,” “us,” or “our”), respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share personal information when you visit our website, communicate with us, or enroll in classes and membership programs at our facility in Winter Springs, Florida.

Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information, depending on how you interact with us:

  • Contact and identity information — such as your name, email address, and phone number when you submit an inquiry, book a trial class, sign up for membership, or communicate with us.
  • Membership and attendance information — such as program selection, class scheduling, billing-related communications, and notes you choose to provide (for example, experience level or health considerations relevant to training).
  • Communications preferences — such as whether you opt in to marketing or transactional SMS, where applicable.
  • Technical data — such as browser type, device information, and usage data collected through cookies or similar technologies on our website (see your browser settings to manage cookies).

How we use your information

We use personal information for legitimate business purposes, including to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support;
  • Schedule classes, manage memberships, and operate our gym services;
  • Send operational messages related to your membership, scheduling, or account (for example, confirmations or important notices);
  • Send marketing communications when you have opted in or as otherwise permitted by law;
  • Improve our website, services, and member experience;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the safety of our members, staff, and facility.

SMS text messages

If you provide a mobile number and opt in to receive SMS messages from us, you understand that message frequency may vary, message and data rates may apply, and you may opt out at any time as described in the consent language presented at signup (for example, by replying STOP where applicable, or by contacting us using the information below). We do not sell your phone number.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our business (for example, payment processing, scheduling or gym management software, email delivery, or analytics), subject to appropriate safeguards. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, members, or the public.

Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your choices

You may opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in those messages, where available. To opt out of promotional SMS, follow the instructions provided in those messages or contact us. You may also contact us to ask questions about your information or to submit requests as required by applicable law.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 in a manner that collects personal information online without appropriate parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child inappropriately, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the effective date above.
